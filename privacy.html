Privacy Policy of EarlyEarn.io

Join EarlyEarn and get your project exposed to our network instantly!

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy informs you about the personal data collected by EarlyEarn.io, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data. By using our platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Controller

EarlyEarn.io is the data controller responsible for processing personal data. If you have any questions regarding data protection, you can contact us via our Contact Form on the website.

3. Data Collected

We collect the following types of personal data:

Registration Data: Name, email address, wallet address

Usage Data: IP address, browser type, access times, device type, operating system

Payment Information: Wallet transaction data (all payments are processed through the blockchain and are publicly visible)

KYC Data (if required): Identity verification, proof of residence

Automatically Collected Data: Log files, referring website URLs, interactions with our platform

4. Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR & CCPA)

Our processing of personal data is based on the following legal grounds:

Contract Fulfillment (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): To provide our platform and process payments.

Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): For fraud prevention, analysis, and platform improvement.

Legal Obligations (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): For KYC verification and anti-money laundering compliance.

Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR, CCPA): For cookies, tracking tools, and marketing purposes.

5. Purpose of Data Processing

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the platform
  • Processing payments and commission payouts
  • Compliance with legal regulations, especially anti-money laundering (AML) requirements
  • Fraud and abuse prevention
  • Customer support and communication
  • Analyzing usage to optimize the platform
  • Ensuring compliance with advertising laws

6. Data Storage and Deletion

We store personal data only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or as required by law. KYC data is deleted once no legal retention obligation exists.

Data of U.S. users is stored in accordance with CCPA guidelines and will be deleted upon request within 45 days.

7. Data Sharing

Your data is not sold to third parties. Data sharing occurs only in the following cases:

Legal Obligations: With government authorities or regulatory bodies.

Service Providers: Hosting, payment processing, KYC providers.

Security Measures: In cases of suspected fraud or security incidents.

Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.

8. Data Transfers Outside the EU & USA

If personal data is processed outside the EU or USA, EarlyEarn.io ensures compliance with appropriate security measures (e.g., standard contractual clauses under GDPR Art. 46).

9. Blockchain Transparency

All payments are processed via blockchain and are publicly visible. Users acknowledge and accept this transparency.

10. Tracking Tools, Cookies & Consent Requirements

We use cookies and tracking tools for analytics and platform optimization.

Opt-in for EU Users (GDPR): EU users must actively consent to non-essential cookies.

Opt-out for US Users (CCPA): US users can opt out of personal data processing for marketing purposes.

Do Not Track (DNT) Signals: Our platform does not currently respond to DNT signals from web browsers.

Settings can be adjusted via cookie banners or browser settings.

11. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, including encryption, firewalls, and access controls.

12. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to the processing of your data (especially for marketing purposes).
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability, Art. 20 GDPR).
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority.
  • Opt-out under CCPA: U.S. users can opt out of data sharing for marketing purposes.

13. Child Protection (COPPA, if applicable)

Our platform is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If a parent discovers that a child under 13 has provided personal data, please contact us for data deletion.

14. Changes to the Privacy Policy

EarlyEarn.io reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. The latest version is always available on our platform. Users will be notified of material changes through platform notifications or direct communication.

15. Contact & Data Protection Inquiries

For privacy-related inquiries, please use our Contact Form on the website.
